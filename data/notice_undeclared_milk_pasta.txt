FDA FOOD ALLERGEN RECALL — JULY 15, 2026

Midwest Grains Co-op of Sacramento, California is voluntarily recalling 20 lb cases of dry pasta because the product may contain milk that is not declared on the label.

The affected product was produced between June 15, 2026 and June 30, 2026 and bears lot code MWG-41. The cases were distributed to food banks and institutional customers in California.

People who have an allergy or severe sensitivity to milk risk a serious or life-threatening allergic reaction if they consume this product. Customers should not consume, serve, or further distribute the affected pasta. Quarantine remaining cases and contact the supplier for disposition instructions.
