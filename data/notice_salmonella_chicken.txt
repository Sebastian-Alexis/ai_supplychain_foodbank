FDA FOOD SAFETY RECALL — JULY 15, 2026

Pacific Frozen Foods of Fremont, California is voluntarily recalling 40 lb cases of frozen chicken quarters because they may be contaminated with Salmonella.

The affected product was produced between June 25, 2026 and July 7, 2026 and bears lot code PFF-514. The cases were distributed to food banks, institutional kitchens, and foodservice customers in California.

Salmonella can cause serious and sometimes fatal infections in young children, frail or elderly people, and others with weakened immune systems. Customers should not consume, serve, or further distribute the affected product. Quarantine remaining cases and contact the supplier for disposition instructions.
